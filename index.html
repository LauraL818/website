Long Shot is a two player sports trivia game aimed to both challenge and entertain the avid sports fan. The two players take turns answering a variety of sports questions until one player scores three goals and comes out victorious.

Long Shot

Baseball Journal is driven towards enhancing a baseball lover's stadium experience. The app gives users a space to store memories and photos from stadiums they have been to as well as search basic facts about each stadium.

Baseball Journal

VentCal is an app that is targeted towards people looking for events in their area. Using the Yelp, EventFul, SeatGeek, and SoundCloud APIs, users can search for events in their area and add events that they like to their calendar.

VentCal

Movie Index provides movie data and information in a more dynamic and interactive way than other sites of its kind. The app allows users to search for films, view basic information, and receive a financial breakdown of each film.

Movie Index
